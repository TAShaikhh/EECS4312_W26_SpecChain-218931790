# Requirement ID: AR1

- Description: [The system shall provide a free version that allows users to track their mood and emotions without requiring payment.]
- Source Persona: [Mental Health Support User]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given a user with no premium features access, When they open the app, Then they can track their mood and emotions without being prompted for payment.]

# Requirement ID: AR2

- Description: [The system shall minimize repetitive questions when gathering user information for insights and advice.]
- Source Persona: [Mental Health Support User]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given a user who has previously entered their mood and emotions, When they request insights or advice, Then the system does not ask redundant questions.]

# Requirement ID: AR3

- Description: [The system shall allow the user to track their emotions and mental state over time.]
- Source Persona: [Mindful Maven]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the user has logged in, When they navigate to the tracking section, Then they can record their emotions and mental state.]

# Requirement ID: AR4

- Description: [The system shall provide the user with insights into their weekly progress.]
- Source Persona: [Mindful Maven]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the user has logged their emotions and mental state for a week, When they view their progress, Then they can see a summary of their weekly mental health trends.]

# Requirement ID: AR5

- Description: [The system shall allow the MoodMonitor to record their mood at multiple points in time.]
- Source Persona: [MoodMonitor]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the MoodMonitor has access to the system, When they choose to record their mood, Then the system shall store the recorded mood with a timestamp.]

# Requirement ID: AR6

- Description: [The system shall provide the MoodMonitor with a view of their mood over time.]
- Source Persona: [MoodMonitor]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the MoodMonitor has recorded their mood at multiple points in time, When they request to view their mood history, Then the system shall display a chronological list or graph of their recorded moods.]

# Requirement ID: AR7

# Requirement ID: AR8
