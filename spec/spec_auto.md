# Requirement ID: AR1

- Description: [The system shall provide free access to basic mood and feeling tracking features for Mental Health Support Users.]
- Source Persona: [Mental Health Support User]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given a user with a free account, when they access the app, then they can track their daily moods and feelings without being prompted to upgrade.]

# Requirement ID: AR2

- Description: [The system shall display a list of free or low-cost mental health resources for Mental Health Support Users.]
- Source Persona: [Mental Health Support User]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A1]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given a user seeking mental health resources, when they navigate to a resources section, then they can view a list of free or low-cost resources without requiring a premium subscription.]

# Requirement ID: AR3

- Description: [The system shall allow the user to track their emotions and mental state over time.]
- Source Persona: [Mindful Maven]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the user has logged in, When they navigate to the tracking section, Then they can record their emotions and mental state.]

# Requirement ID: AR4

- Description: [The system shall provide the user with insights into their weekly progress.]
- Source Persona: [Mindful Maven]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A2]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the user has tracked their emotions and mental state for a week, When they view their progress, Then they can see a summary of their weekly trends.]

# Requirement ID: AR5

- Description: [The system shall allow the MoodMonitor to record their mood on a daily basis.]
- Source Persona: [MoodMonitor]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the MoodMonitor has access to the system, When they choose to record their mood, Then the system shall accept and store the mood entry with a date.]

# Requirement ID: AR6

- Description: [The system shall provide the MoodMonitor with a view of their mood entries over time.]
- Source Persona: [MoodMonitor]
- Traceability: [Derived from review group A3]
- Acceptance Criteria: [Given the MoodMonitor has made multiple mood entries, When they request to view their mood history, Then the system shall display a list or graph of their mood entries in chronological order.]
